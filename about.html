Welcome!

Hi, my name is Fiacre Rougieux. Welcome to this introduction to solar cell. I am a senior lecturer at the University of New South Wales. I have PhD from the Australian National University in the field of photovoltaics and semiconductor materials. The work of my research team lies at the intersection of materials science, nanotechnology, solid-state chemistry, solar cells and semiconductor physics. We focus on developing the materials and devices required to accelerate our energy transition.

The animations use p5.js. The source code we have written for the animations are all open source and can be found on our git repo.
